A Legacy of Leadership

Namilyango College was founded in 1902 and it was the first boys' boarding school in Uganda and was originally intended to educate the sons of chiefs. As Uganda’s premier secondary institution for boys, Namilyango College was established by the Mill Hill Missionaries. Our purpose has always been clear and that is to nurture young men grounded in the firm pillars of faith, discipline, and excellence.

The Hill of Excellence

Perched upon the serene Namilyango Hill within the Mukono District, our campus offers a unique environment that blends a century of rich tradition with modern academic standards. The college is recognized for its excellence in academics and was a pioneer in information technology in Ugandan schools, establishing one of the first computer labs.

The Nisi Dominus Spirit

Guided by our motto, Nisi Dominus (“Without the Lord”), we are dedicated to a holistic education. We believe in shaping the intellect, refining the character, and fostering a heart for service in every student. Founded on a Christian foundation, we strive to develop well-rounded individuals who can contribute positively to society.

Leadership & Sports

Beyond the classroom, our community thrives in leadership training, cultural expression, and competitive sports. We are particularly recognized as a national dominant force in rugby, a sport that embodies our strength and teamwork where we have won numerous national titles. The college also has a strong tradition of student leadership through its student council.

Our Global Impact

At Namilyango College, our goal transcends basic education. We inspire young men to transform into principled leaders equipped to make a significant impact on Uganda and the global stage. The college has produced many distinguished alumni who have excelled in various fields such as politics, academia, and business.
