Welcome to Namilyango College

A leading Catholic boys' boarding school nurturing disciplined, academically strong, and socially responsible young men.

What do we offer?

Namilyango College stands as Uganda’s premier historic school for young men that combines a century of heritage with a forward-thinking approach to modern education.Under our guiding principle, Nisi Dominus ("Without the Lord"), we focus on more than just grades. We cultivate principled leaders through a balance of rigorous academics, spiritual growth, and a legendary sports culture. Our mission is to transform students into disciplined, high-achieving men ready to lead and serve on a global scale.

Motto

Nisi Dominus — "Without God, we can do nothing."

Location

Seeta, Mukono District, Uganda. A calm and structured boarding setting for meaningful learning.

School Focus

Character formation, academic strength, and responsible leadership.
